Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Silly Money Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Usage data (clicks, earnings, level progress)
  • Offer completion status
  • IP address and location data

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process your earnings and withdrawals
  • To verify task completions and prevent fraud
  • To improve user experience and app performance
  • To communicate important updates

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and encrypted during transmission.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for:

  • Offer wall providers (task verification)
  • Payment processors (PayPal, gift cards)
  • Analytics (app performance)
  • Ad networks (optional rewards)

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data as specified in their respective terms.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data
  • Disable data collection (limited app functionality)

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately for deletion.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
